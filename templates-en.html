Your comprehensive guide to understanding and creating WhatsApp templates efficiently

What are WhatsApp Templates?

WhatsApp templates are pre-approved messages by Meta (WhatsApp). These templates are used to send messages to customers outside the 24-hour window, or to send automated messages and important notifications. Templates help businesses communicate with their customers in an organized and professional manner.

Template Types

Marketing

Used to promote products and services, send special offers, and announce events.

Utility

Used to provide useful information to customers such as account updates, security settings, and system notifications.

Important Note on Meta Policy

Marketing Templates: Subject to strict review by Meta and approval process may take 24 to 48 hours. Marketing content must be useful and not annoying to users. Avoid using words like "free", "limited offer", or any content that might be considered spam.

Information and Utility Templates: Usually approved faster as they provide important information to customers.

Important Tips for Creating Templates

  • Use clear and polite language
  • Avoid spelling and grammatical errors
  • Do not exceed 1024 characters per template
  • Use dynamic variables to customize messages
  • Ensure content provides real value to the customer
  • Test the template before sending it for review
  • Keep a backup of important templates
